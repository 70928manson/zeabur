# Deploy RSSHub

Deploy Your RSSHub in Zeabur.

### Step1: Fork RSSHub Repo

Open the [official repository](https://github.com/DIYgod/RSSHub) of RSSHub.

Click fork to fork the repository to your own account.

### Step2: Deploy RSSHub App

Click on **Deploy service** or **Add new service** button, then choose **Deploy your source code**.

![deploy](/framework/deploy.png)

Search for your RSSHub repository, click on import, your RSSHub will start deploying.

### Step3: Add Domain for RSSHub

After the deployment is complete, you need to add a domain for your RSSHub.

You can learn how to add a domain [here](/en-US/deploy/domain-binding)
