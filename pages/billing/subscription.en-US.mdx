# Subscription

## What is Subscription?

There are 3 types of plans on Zeabur: **Free Plan**, **Developer Plan** and **Team Plan**.
You can check the details of each plan on [plans](/billing/plans) page.

If you has not subscribed to any plan, you'll be considered as a **Free Plan** user by default.

## How to Subscribe?

Zeabur's subscription is based on [Stripe](https://stripe.com/).

There are 2 ways to subscribe to a plan: **Credit Card** and **Alipay**.

### Subscribe with Credit Card

You can subscribe to a plan with your credit card, paying in USD.

- Setup without subscription: Go to [billing page](https://dash.zeabur.com/account/billing), you can setup your credit card in **Payment Method** section. After you've input your credit card information, click **Add** button to save your credit card, then you can simply click **Subscribe** button in [plan page](https://dash.zeabur.com/update-plan) to update your subscription.
- Setup with subscription: Go to the [plan page](https://dash.zeabur.com/update-plan) in dashboard, choose a plan, and then input your credit card information, click **Subscribe** button, you'll be redirected to Stripe's checkout page, fill the form and you'll be subscribed to the plan with your credit card saved.

### Subscribe with Alipay

If you don't have a credit card that is compatible with Stripe service, you can subscribe to a plan with your Alipay account, paying in CNY.

Go to the [plan page](https://dash.zeabur.com/update-plan) in dashboard, choose a plan and click **No Credit Card?** link, you'll be redirected to Stripe's checkout page, where you can setup your subscription with Alipay.

> **Note**: Once you've subscribed to a plan with Alipay, you can't subscribe with your credit card anymore.

## How to Downgrade to Free Plan

If you've subscribed to a paid plan, you can downgrade to **Free Plan** at any time.

Go to the [plan page](https://dash.zeabur.com/update-plan) in dashboard, choose **Free Plan** and click **Subscribe** button, you'll be redirected to Stripe's checkout page, fill the form and you'll be downgraded to **Free Plan**.
